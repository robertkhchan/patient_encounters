3) All of the patient that have been admitted in between July 5th, 2014 and August 19, 2014

Query
==========
 select distinct p.*
 from patients as p 
 join encounters as e on p.id=e.patient_id 
 where e.admitted_at >= '2014-07-15' 
 and e.admitted_at < '2014-08-20';
